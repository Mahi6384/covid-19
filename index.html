    Stay Home, Stay Safe

    Take Steps to Protect Yourself

    Always wear a Mask

    When you wear a mask, you protect others
    as well as yourself.

    Wash your Hands

    Handwashing with soap remains one of our
    best defenses against the virus,

    Keep Distance

    Maintaizn at 2-metre distance between yourself
    and others to reduce your risk of infection

    Main Symptoms of Covid-19

    • Headache
    • Dry Cough
    • Shaking-Chills
    • Fever
    • Sore Throat
    • Shortness of Breath

    Wash your Hands in 10 steps.

    • Step 1 :Rinse Your Hands
    • Step 2 :Take HandWash
    • Step 3 :Rub Hands Palm to Palm
    • Step 4 :Between Fingers on one side
    • Step 5 :Between Fingers on opposite side
    • Step 6 :Rub the back of fingers
    • Step 7 :Wash fingertips
    • Step 8 :Rub each wrist on opposite site
    • Step 9 :Clean Thumbs
    • Step 10 :Rinse your Hands
